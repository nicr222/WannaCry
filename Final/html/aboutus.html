About Us

Welcome to CWB Fight Club, premier fight club in Stevens Point since the '90s, dedicated to combat sports. Experience top-notch training in Brazilian jiu-jitsu, karate, and Muay Thai led by our highly experienced owner.

At CWB Fight Club, we believe in the transformative power of combat sports

Beyond physical training, we understand that martial arts instill discipline, mental resilience, and self-confidence. Whether you are a seasoned fighter or a beginner taking your first steps into the world of combat sports, our club welcomes individuals from all backgrounds and skill levels. At our Fight Club:

  • Earn improved physical fitness and conditioning through intense combat sports training.
  • Acquire enhanced self-defense skills in Brazilian jiu-jitsu, karate, and Muay Thai.
  • Develop mental resilience, self-confidence, and a positive mindset within the supportive martial arts community.

Join us at CWB Fight Club and experience the thrill of combat sports while honing your skills and unlocking your full potential. Our state-of-the-art facility, world-class instructors, and passionate community are ready to help you embark on a transformative journey in the world of martial arts. Are you ready to unleash your inner warrior? Come and be a part of CWB Fight Club, where the pursuit of excellence and personal growth converge.
